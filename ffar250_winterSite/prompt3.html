MOTHER / EarthBound Beginnings

This GIF depicts the opening battle of the RPG MOTHER/Earthbound Beginnings, in which a poltergeist invades the house of the protagonist, NInten and possesses his lamp. This GIF is an example of Framing Home, as Ninten’s once peaceful house has quickly turned into a hostile environment, until he can get the situation under control. Ninten’s predicament is similar to the one the protagonist of my final project story faces: a supernatural force upsets their way of life and has to be stopped.
