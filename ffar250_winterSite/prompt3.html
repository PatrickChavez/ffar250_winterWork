MOTHER / EarthBound Beginnings

This GIF depicts the opening scene of the game MOTHER/Earthbound Beginnings, in which a poltergeist invades the house of the protagonist, NInten (the boy in the stripped shirt) and starts to possess his furniture. This GIF is an example of Framing Home, as Ninten’s once peaceful house has quickly turned into a hostile environment, until he can get the situation under control. Ninten’s predicament is similar to the one the protagonist of my final project story faces: a supernatural force upsets their way of life and has to be stopped.
